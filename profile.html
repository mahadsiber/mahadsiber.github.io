General Profile

Description: Ma'had al-Hadlarah as-Siberniyah (MHS) is a cultural studio based on pesantren with transformative Islamic values, functioning as a cooperative learning and working space.

Vision: Realizing an adaptive and inclusive Indonesian Islamic society towards the power of emerging technology.

Mission:

  • Educate humans and AI to form a social movement coherent with Ahlus-Sunnah wal-Jama'ah An-Nahdliyah values.
  • Build an impactful and sustainable Innovator Hub.

Program Development

Halaqah Syumuliyah Islamiyah

  • Holistic Approach
  • Collaboration and Discussion
  • Standardization and Documentation
  • Technology Integration
  • Evaluation and Assessment

Content & Objective: Islam and Indonesian Social Sciences, Islam and State Administration, Islam and Environment

Project Development

  • MHS AI: Knowledge Management System
  • MHS Research & Innovation Management: Platform integrating Design Thinking, Knowledge Management, and Innovation Management
  • MHS Journal & Media: Emerging Tech Amplification Strategy
  • MHS Innovator Ecosystem: Collaborative platform for MHS graduates

Milestones

Objective: Launch MHS as an education and research center for new technology by June 2025

Key Results:

  • Build physical and digital infrastructure by February 2025
  • Develop and launch Halaqah Syumuliyah Islamiyah program by June 2025
  • Build and test AI-based KMS prototype by May 2025
  • Publish 4 editions of MHS Journal & Media before June 2025
